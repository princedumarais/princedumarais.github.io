PRINCE DU MARAIS

Our Story

PRINCE DU MARAIS, “Prince of the Marsh” in French, is an independent organic clothing and accessories brand from France. Our emblem is the frog prince styled as the royal fleur-de-lys, which embodies the spirit of the brand: Organic and Chic.

We create clothing and home accessories using only 100% organic natural fabrics such as organic peace silk, velvety Aegean cotton, and virgin wool from Pantagonia. By using the best organic fabrics available, we make sure that we are part of the global initiative to produce garments with environmentally sound and socially responsible methods.

In respect of the natural beauty of the fabrics and as a token of our love for simplicity, we keep our designs pure and timeless. Our garments are developed in-house from pattern-making to sampling to the finishing touches. We believe that comfort and style should co-exist. A PRINCE DU MARAIS creation is for the truly discerning.

From the tiny ones to the young at heart, we would like to invite you to become part of our story, experience Organic and Exquisite.
